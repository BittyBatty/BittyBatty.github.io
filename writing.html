Untitled Vampire Book 1

Riccardo Tenepri and Arthur Seavers find themselves in the middle of a world-changing plot when they commit themselves to getting a kidnapped fae, Leonardo Dupont, back home. It's a task much easier said than done when Riccardo's genocidal father is enraged to find Riccardo has smuggled his little brother, Erik, on this journey as well. A genocidal vampire king is not the only obstacle in their path, though. The mystery of why Leon was kidnapped and gifted to Riccardo in the first place hangs over their heads, as well as what happened to the rest of the fae in the first place.
