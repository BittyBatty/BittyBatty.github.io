Leonardo Dupont is one of the last fae to survive the centuries-long extermination of his people led by vampires. When he's captured and gifted to the Tenepri heir as a trophy, Leon expects on cruelty. However, Prince Riccardo Tenepri is nothing like the monsters who came before him. Quiet, gentle, and desperate to escape the violence of his family's legacy, he sees Leon not as a prize, but as someone worth saving.

Riccardo's father, King Demetri, has darker plans. Leon's blood carries power enough to create a new breed of unstoppable vampire soldier, and he'll tear the world apart to possess it. With the king's ruthless enforcer on their trail, Riccardo and Leon flee the only life the prince has ever known.

But even if it costs him everything, Riccardo has sworn to get Leon home. Should he fail, Leon will become a weapon of war, and Riccardo will be killed for treason against the crown.
