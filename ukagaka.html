FAQ's

A Ukagaka, or ghost, is a desktop widget usually in the form of a character that the user can interact. The most common interactions include giving them gifts, asking them questions, dressing them up, and petting them.

A balloon is the box that dialogue and the left-click menu appears in. These are stylized and often customized for each individual ghost and it's recommended you download them as well.

The first thing you'll need to do to download any of the below Ukagakas is download a program called SSP for Windows. You'll see a large pink button on the left with Japanese on it and in tiny letters "complete install package". Click this and install it. After you've successfully installed them, a character will appear named Emily. At this point, you can drag the .nar files of my ghosts and balloons you've downloaded onto Emily and she will install them. Afterwards, right click Emily to pull up another menu, click the 'change ghost' option in the menu, and switch to the ghost of your choosing.
